Privacy Policy for FairTrust-AI

FairTrust-AI is a bias detection and explainability SaaS platform designed to help teams deploy responsible AI systems. We are committed to transparent data handling and practical privacy protections.

Sections

1. Introduction 2. Data We Collect 3. How We Use Data 4. Data Storage 5. Data Security 6. Data Sharing 7. User Rights 8. Cookies 9. Compliance Awareness 10. Contact

1. Introduction

FairTrust-AI provides fairness auditing workflows for ML systems, including dataset review, bias detection, SHAP/LIME explainability, and stakeholder reports. This policy explains what information we process and how we protect it while enabling responsible AI governance.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: primarily email address when account features are used.
  • User-provided datasets: files uploaded for fairness analysis and report generation.
  • Minimal usage metadata: technical logs such as request timing, error traces, and session diagnostics for platform reliability.

3. How We Use Data

  • Run fairness, bias, and explainability evaluations you request.
  • Generate developer, regulator, executive, and end-user reports.
  • Improve product reliability and workflow quality based on aggregate platform behavior, not personal dataset content.
We do NOT use your datasets to train our models.

4. Data Storage

By default, uploaded files are processed in temporary runtime workflows. Where persistence features are enabled, retention is user-controlled and can be minimized or disabled according to your operational needs. Data in transit and at rest should be protected with modern encryption controls.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption for data transmission and storage paths where applicable.
  • Controlled access principles for operational and admin workflows.
  • Secure processing practices for evaluation jobs and report generation.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share user data for advertising or third-party monetization. Data is processed only for service delivery and technical support operations directly tied to your use of FairTrust-AI.

7. User Rights

  • Request access to information associated with your account.
  • Request deletion of uploaded data where technically feasible.
  • Control storage preferences where optional retention settings are available.

8. Cookies

FairTrust-AI currently uses no or minimal cookie-based tracking. If cookie use expands in future releases (for example, secure session continuity or analytics), this policy will be updated accordingly.

9. Compliance Awareness

FairTrust-AI is built with awareness of global privacy expectations including GDPR and CCPA principles. We do not claim blanket legal compliance for every customer context; each organization should validate obligations based on its jurisdiction and data practices.

10. Contact

For privacy inquiries, data requests, or policy clarifications, contact us at fairtrustai@gmail.com.
